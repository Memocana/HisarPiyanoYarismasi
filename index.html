First Hisar School International Piano Competition

19-21 June 2017

The primary goals of our competition open to pianist of 6-18 ages are:

  • To Strengthen the artistic ties of students
  • To discover new talents in piano performance
  • To promote classical piano music as it is one of the important components for the cultural education of the rising generation
  • To encourage talented pianists who are at the beginning of their careers
  • To connect with national-international music schools and teachers
