First Hisar School International Piano Competition


Thank you for your interest in our competition. Finalists will be anounced at May 15th 2017, 11.00 am (GMT+3) in the competition website.

Hisar School First International Piano Competition

June 19-21, 2017

The competition, open for pianist between age 6 to 18, is aimed to:

  • strengthen the artistic ties of students,
  • connect with national-international music schools and teachers,
  • create an environment for sharing,
  • discover new talents in piano performance,
  • promote classical piano music as it is one of the important components for the cultural education of the rising generation,
  • encourage talented pianists who are at the beginning of their careers.
    • In this competition, participants will be given the opportunity to take the stage, challenge themselves, gain experience in a platform other than their own environment, and experience the enthusiasm and passion of the amateur spirit
